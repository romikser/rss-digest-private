Best Time to Run

Recommended Window

10:00 – 13:00 is the sweet spot. Any morning drizzle should ease by mid-morning as the front moves through. Temperatures will be comfortable in the high teens, and the afternoon risk of renewed showers increases after 15:00. If you prefer an early start, the window from 07:00 – 09:00 is also workable — just expect some light drizzle and cooler air around 16°C.

What to Wear

  • Lightweight moisture-wicking base layer — temps are mild but the wind will cut
  • Light windproof jacket or gilet — SW–W gusts up to 30 km/h on exposed routes
  • Shorts or lightweight tights — 16°C+ feels fine once you're moving
  • Running cap — keeps drizzle off your face and shields from the low morning sun
  • Skip the waterproof shell — drizzle is light; breathability matters more

Heads Up

Dublin in July means "partly anything" — cloud and sun alternate quickly. The southwest wind will be in your face on southbound legs, so plan your route to tackle it early while you're fresh and have the tailwind on the way home. Phoenix Park and the Dodder path stay sheltered compared to the coast or open canal stretches. Paths may be slightly damp from overnight drizzle — watch footing on packed gravel. Nothing here should cancel the run; just layer right and go.
