Dublin Weekend · Heads-Up Sat 4 Jul – Sun 5 Jul
Weather Events Things To Do
Weekend Weather

Saturday looks decent — partly cloudy with a high of 21°C and barely any rain. Sunday warms up a touch to 23°C under overcast skies but stays dry, making it a good outdoor weekend overall for Dublin.

Events to Know About
Longitude Festival 2026
Marlay Park · Sat 4 & Sun 5 Jul
Dublin's biggest outdoor music festival returns for two days of hip-hop, R&B, electronic and pop across multiple stages. 40,000 fans per day at the beautiful grounds of Marlay Park in Rathfarnham.
Take That – The Circus Live
Aviva Stadium · Sat 4 Jul
Take That bring their grand theatrical Circus Live production to Aviva Stadium — the final date of their 2026 UK & Ireland tour. Expect spectacular staging, circus performers, and all the hits.
The Scratch
Iveagh Gardens · Sat 4 Jul, 8pm
Ireland's fiercest live act — guitar virtuosos who blur fingerstyle folk and metal — headline Dublin's beloved open-air garden series. One of the must-see homegrown acts of the summer.
Elvis Costello & The Imposters + Charlie Sexton
Iveagh Gardens · Sun 5 Jul, 6pm
Radio Soul! — The Early Songs of Elvis Costello tour draws from classics spanning My Aim Is True (1977) to Blood & Chocolate (1986). A rare deep-dive into the early catalogue, live in a garden setting.
Flavours of Fingal County Show
Newbridge House & Farm, Donabate · Sat 4 & Sun 5 Jul
14th year of this family favourite — local food & drink producers, equestrian displays, live music, artisan stalls, and kids' activities set in the historic parklands of Newbridge House, just north of the city.
Things to Do
▶Dún Laoghaire Pier Walk — With temps hitting 21–23°C and winds staying light, a stroll along the East or West Pier is as good as Dublin gets. Grab a coffee at the end, watch the yachts, and swing by the Sunday Farmer's Market on the way back.
▶Phoenix Park Cycle — Hire a bike and explore Europe's largest urban park. The deer are reliably grazing in the south grounds and the Walled Kitchen Garden makes a good mid-ride stop. Best done Saturday morning before the afternoon crowd arrives.
