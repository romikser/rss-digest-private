What's On

Longitude Festival 2026

Sat & Sun · Marlay Park, Rathfarnham · All day from ~2pm

Dublin's flagship outdoor music festival returns for two days of hip-hop, R&B, electronic and pop across multiple stages. Multi-day tickets are typically €190–210 and tend to sell out in advance — check Ticketmaster IE if you haven't grabbed yours yet.

Take That — 2026 Tour

Saturday 4 July · Aviva Stadium, Lansdowne Road · Evening

The iconic pop group bring their 2026 stadium tour to Aviva Stadium. Expect production-heavy nostalgia from one of the UK's biggest-selling acts. Large crowd expected — allow extra time getting in and out of Lansdowne Road.

Dún Laoghaire Summerfest — Opening Weekend

Sat & Sun (runs 3–12 July) · Various venues, Dún Laoghaire

The opening weekend of Summerfest features dlr Streets — live music and street performance at venues across the town including The Purty Kitchen, Walters, and Volume Records. Acts span Kíla, Bagatelle, The 4 of Us, and tribute acts for Abba, Fleetwood Mac, The Doors and more. Many events are free; ticketed gigs from a few euros.

Flavours of Fingal County Show

Sat & Sun · Newbridge House & Farm, Donabate · All day

The 14th edition of this family-friendly show celebrates farming, food, music, sport and rural life set against the grounds of Newbridge Demesne. Good craic for kids and adults alike, and a pleasant escape from the city if the weather plays along.

Things to Do

Cycle or run in Phoenix Park (if Saturday stays dry)

With highs near 22 °C and some sunshine forecast for Saturday, the Park is at its best. Hire a bike near the main gate or bring your own — the 8 km perimeter loop is flat and gives you deer, monuments and green space without ever leaving Dublin. Good option before or instead of the festival crowds.

National Gallery of Ireland (Sunday contingency)

If Sunday's showers materialise, the National Gallery on Merrion Square is free, warm and genuinely world-class — from Caravaggio to Jack B. Yeats. Pair it with a walk around the Georgian squares and lunch in Baggot Street or the surrounding area. No booking required.
