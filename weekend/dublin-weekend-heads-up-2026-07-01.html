Dún Laoghaire Summerfest
Various venues, Dún Laoghaire · Saturday & Sunday
10-day festival (3–12 Jul) with 70+ acts — live music, street performance, tribute bands and local groups spread across pubs, churches and outdoor spaces in Dún Laoghaire.
Flavours of Fingal County Show — Last Day
Newbridge House & Farm, Donabate · Saturday
14th year of this family-friendly county show combining farming, artisan food, live music, sport and rural entertainment on the beautiful Newbridge estate.

Things to Do

Dún Laoghaire Pier & Forty Foot (if there's a dry spell) A walk along the East Pier and a look at the Forty Foot bathing spot is a classic Dublin Saturday — even in drizzle it's worth it, and the Summerfest has live music nearby to duck into.
Chester Beatty Library (Sunday rain plan) Free admission, world-class collection of manuscripts, prints and decorative arts from across Asia and the Middle East — one of Dublin's most underrated cultural spots, right beside Dublin Castle.
