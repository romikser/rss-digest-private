Things To Do
▶Saturday morning: Grand Canal Docks walk
Saturday looks like the warmer of the two days (19–24°C, some breaks of sun). A stroll along the Grand Canal Docks or Docklands waterfront makes the most of any dry spells before the afternoon events kick off.
▶Sunday fallback: National Museum of Ireland — Natural History
Sunday is cloudier with patchy drizzle expected. The Natural History Museum (Merrion St, free entry) is a classic Dublin rainy-day option — Victorian "Dead Zoo" vibes, no queues needed. Five-minute walk from Iveagh Gardens if you're heading to Elvis Costello in the evening.
